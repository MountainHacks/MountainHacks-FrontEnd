MountainHacks is the Mountain-West's premier hackathon organization for university students. Come build with us.

MountainHacks is a BYU-based student organization geared towards turning computer science students into hackers.

No, we're not about breaking into systems or finding security exploits. We use the term hacker in it's original meaning - the correct meaning. The term hacker dates back to MIT in the 70's, used to describe anyone who just loves to program and create new things. Hackers know how to get stuff done. Need that new web platform done by Monday? A hacker will get it done by Friday. We 'hack' together APIs and platforms to build the next big products off tomorrow.
