MountainHacks


The mountain-west's first university hackathon.

Feb. 27-28th 2015

MountainHacks is the Mountain-West's premier 24-hour hackathon for university students.
Come build with us.

So how does this work?

You have 24 hours to build a new unique product, whether it be an iOS app, an Android app, a web app, a platform, a hardware hack... etc. The only rule is that it has to be an original product. All your programming must take place at the hackathon, however you may come up with ideas and form teams prior to the event.

What should I bring?

Just bring you, your laptop, and your awesome coding skills. Some optional items you can bring are pillows, headphones, small whiteboards, a travel size blanket, and anything else you need to be comfortable hacking!

Am I locked in?

Not at all! You are free to leave the venue whenever you like or need to.

Ok, how much is this going to cost?

It's FREE! All expenses including food, prizes, giveaways, venue space, travel costs (where applicable) are all covered by our awesome sponsors!

How will my hack get judged?

We will bring in a number of professionals in the field, including engineers and professors, to evaluate your hack on a number of considerations. We will be giving awards for Best iOS App, Best Hardware Hack, Most Technically Impressive, etc. so that every hack has a chance to win.

Will there be anything else to do?

You bet! We'll be having a number of activities throughout the night including workshops and coding challenges, as well as non-coding related items like a Super Smash Bro. tournament in case you need a break.
